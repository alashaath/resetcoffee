Born out of a love for great coffee.

Reset Specialty Coffee is a small shop in McAllen, Texas. We source carefully, brew intentionally, and believe every cup should be something worth pausing for.

Our story

A place to slow down and reset.

We started Reset because we believed McAllen deserved a specialty coffee experience — one that takes the craft seriously without taking itself too seriously.

Our name says it all. Whatever you're carrying when you walk in, we want you to feel like you can set it down for a moment. Reset. Breathe. Have something beautiful to drink.

We work exclusively with specialty-grade beans scored above 80 points on the SCA scale — grown with intention, processed with care, and selected for their character.
